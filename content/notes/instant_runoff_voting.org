#+title: Instant Runoff Voting

Instant Runoff Voting is a [[id:895de7be-5ff8-477a-96d7-3bdc0376f248][Voting System]] where voters rank their candidates in order of preference. Then, the following procedure is performed until a candidate reaches 50% of the vote and wins:

- Anyone who voted for the last-place candidate has their vote transferred to their second-choice/third-choice etc.

#+title: Magnetized Target Fusion

Magnetized Target Fusion is a way of performing [[id:bd71fb64-03fd-4b55-90ce-46764a191b34][§Nuclear Fusion]] that combines the concepts of [[id:81fe9d60-597f-42f9-a471-3873a88f3d09][§Magnetic Confinement Fusion]] and [[id:fee6b120-a137-4721-ad6a-ab9abf2dbe3b][§Inertial Confinement Fusion]].

- A spherical shell of molten lead-lithium is used to compress hydrogen [[id:e5eaef1b-c26a-42c3-9525-99c10c4d8035][§Plasma]] to the temperature required for fusion.

- The heat captured by this metal is then used to run a steam turbine.

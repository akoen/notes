*There were fewer than two violent crimes reported per police officer in 2019.*
According to the census ( [[file:data/police_reform/2019_state_local.xls]]), there were 711,583 full-time U.S. police officers with the power of arrest in 2019. According to the [[https://web.archive.org/web/20210603181749/https://ucr.fbi.gov/crime-in-the-u.s/2019/crime-in-the-u.s.-2019/topic-pages/tables/table-1][FBI]], there were 1,203,808 violent crimes reported that year.

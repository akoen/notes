#+title: Writing is Thinking

- [[file:writing.org][§Writing]] is the process by which ideas are transformed into concepts.
  
- [[file:sönke_ahrens.org][§Sönke Ahrens]] argues that thinking is done on paper. It is not merely a record of thought. [[file:writing.org][§Writing]] is thinking. [[file:books/How-to-Take-Smart-Notes.org][#§How to Take Smart Notes]]

  - [[file:writing.org][§Writing]] provides us with the distance required to analyze an argument. Otherwise, it would occupy the same mental resources required for this scrutiny.
